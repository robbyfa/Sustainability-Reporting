Sustainability Reporting

Welcome to our sustainable reporting platform, crafted in accordance with the EU taxonomy. Navigate through the four pages: the introduction home page, the interactive criteria finder, the code-checking page, and the ontology insights. This collaborative effort by Robert, Amir, Romke, Rhea, and Irem is based on Semmtech's information, integrating an ontology for structured data analysis.
